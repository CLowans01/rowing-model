# Christopher Lowans

Energy systems and techno-economic analysis, holding a PhD from Queen's University
Belfast in the socio-techno-economic analysis of energy and transport poverty. My
published work spans energy and transport poverty, the economics of domestic
retrofit, and equitable decarbonisation pathways, with contributions to institutional
analysis including the IEA's Global EV Outlook. This site collects that work alongside
independent analytical projects.

---

## Projects

**Does recovery style affect boat speed? A first-principles model of the rowing stroke**
A physics-based investigation, built from scratch in Python, of whether the timing
of a rower's recovery changes boat speed — motivated by a long-standing disagreement
about rowing technique. Develops a three-stage model from first principles, validates
its drag parameter against published experimental data, and quantifies the result
over a real race course.
[Read the full analysis →](rowing_analysis.ipynb)
